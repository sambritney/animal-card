Soft Coated Wheaten Terrier

Wheatens are known to have dark brown or red coats when they are born. Eventually their hair turns blond, like the color of wheat.

  • Hypoallergenic: Yes
  • Life span: 12 - 15 years
  • Height: Males 18 - 19 in, Females 17 - 18 in
  • Weight: Males 35 - 40 lbs, Females 30 - 35 lbs

The Soft Coated Wheaten Terrier is a pure bread dog originating from Ireland. These dogs have a single coat which sheds very little hair. Wheatens are known to be playful, spirited, faithful, energetic, intelligent and affectionate.
